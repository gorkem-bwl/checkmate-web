## Overview

Export your monitors to backup your configuration or migrate to another Checkmate instance.

## Exporting monitors

1. Navigate to **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Scroll to the **Export monitors** section
3. Click **Export monitors to JSON**
4. Save the downloaded file

<Callout type="info">
Only Admins and Superadmins can export monitors.
</Callout>
