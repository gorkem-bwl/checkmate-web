## Overview

Timezone settings control how dates and times are displayed throughout the application. Set this to your local timezone for easier incident correlation.

## Setting your timezone

1. Navigate to **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Find the **Timezone** section at the top
3. Search for and select your timezone
4. Click **Save** at the bottom of the page
