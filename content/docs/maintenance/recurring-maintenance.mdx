description: "Different scheduling options."

## Overview

Choose between one-time and recurring maintenance windows based on your needs.

## One-time maintenance

Use one-time windows for specific events like deployments, migrations, or emergency repairs. They run once at the scheduled time.

## Recurring maintenance

Use recurring windows for regular maintenance like nightly backups or weekly updates. Options include:

- **Daily**: Runs every day at the specified time
- **Weekly**: Runs every 7 days at the specified time
