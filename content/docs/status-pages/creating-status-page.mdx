## Overview

Status pages provide a public view of your service health. Share them with customers to communicate uptime and incidents transparently.

## Creating a status page

1. Navigate to **Status pages** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create status page**
3. Enter a name for your status page
4. Configure the URL slug (e.g., status.yourcompany.com)
5. Click **Create**

## Adding monitors

After creation, add monitors to display on the status page. Visitors can see the status of each service without accessing your dashboard.
