## Overview

Choose which monitors appear on your status page. You can group related services and display them with custom names.

## Adding monitors

1. Navigate to **Status pages** and select your page
2. Click **Add monitor**
3. Select monitors from the list
4. Optionally set display names
5. Click **Save**

## Organizing monitors

Drag monitors to reorder them. Group related services together for a logical presentation.
