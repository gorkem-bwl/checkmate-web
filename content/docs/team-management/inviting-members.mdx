## Overview

Invite team members to collaborate on monitoring. New members receive an email invitation to join your workspace.

## Sending invitations

1. Click your profile icon and select **Account**
2. Go to the **Team** section
3. Click **Invite member**
4. Enter their email address
5. Select their role (User or Admin)
6. Click **Send invitation**

<Callout type="warn">
Invitations expire after 1 hour. Send a new invitation if the link has expired.
</Callout>
