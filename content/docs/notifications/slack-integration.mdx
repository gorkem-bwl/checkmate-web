## Overview

Slack integration sends incident alerts to your Slack channels using webhooks. Your team can see and discuss issues in real-time.

## Creating a Slack webhook

1. Go to api.slack.com/apps and create a new app
2. Select **Incoming Webhooks** and enable them
3. Click **Add New Webhook to Workspace**
4. Choose the channel for alerts
5. Copy the webhook URL

## Configuring in Checkmate

1. Navigate to **Integrations** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create integration**
3. Select **Slack**
4. Paste your webhook URL
5. Click **Test** to send a test message
6. Click **Save**
