## Overview

The incident timeline shows the progression of an incident from detection to resolution. Use it to understand what happened and when.

## Timeline events

- **Started**: When the first failure was detected
- **Notifications sent**: When alerts were dispatched
- **Acknowledged**: When someone began investigating (if applicable)
- **Resolved**: When the monitor recovered

## Duration tracking

Checkmate calculates incident duration from the first failed check to the first successful check after the incident. This appears in both the timeline and incident summary.
