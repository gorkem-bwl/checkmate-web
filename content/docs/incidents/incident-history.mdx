## Overview

Incident history provides a record of all past outages. Use it to identify patterns, measure reliability, and report on SLA compliance.

## Viewing incident history

1. Navigate to **Incidents** in the sidebar
2. Use filters to narrow by monitor, date range, or status
3. Click an incident to view its full timeline

## Analyzing patterns

Look for these patterns in your incident history:

- **Recurring times**: Incidents at the same time might indicate scheduled jobs or traffic spikes
- **Duration trends**: Longer incidents may indicate slower recovery processes
- **Frequency changes**: Increasing incidents could signal infrastructure problems
