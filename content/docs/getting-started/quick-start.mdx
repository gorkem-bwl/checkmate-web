## Overview

This guide walks you through creating your first monitor and setting up notifications. You'll have a working monitoring setup in about 5 minutes.

## Creating your first monitor

1. Navigate to **Uptime** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create monitor** in the top right
3. Select **HTTP/HTTPS** as the monitor type
4. Enter the URL you want to monitor (e.g., `https://example.com`)
5. Set the check interval (1-5 minutes recommended for critical services)
6. Click **Create monitor** to start monitoring

<Callout type="info">
Use descriptive names like "Production API" or "Marketing Website" to easily identify monitors in alerts.
</Callout>

## Verifying your monitor

After creating a monitor, Checkmate immediately performs the first check. You can verify it's working by:

1. Click on your new monitor to open the details page
2. Check the status indicator shows **Up** (green)
3. View the response time in the metrics panel

## Setting up notifications

To receive alerts when your monitor goes down:

1. Navigate to **Notifications** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create notification**
3. Select your preferred channel (email, Slack, Discord, etc.)
4. Configure the notification settings
5. Click **Test** to verify the notification works
