## Overview

Filter and navigate through your check history to find specific results or analyze patterns over time.

## Date range filtering

Select from preset date ranges to narrow your view:

- **Recent**: The most recent checks (default view)
- **Day**: Last 24 hours of checks
- **Week**: Last 7 days of checks
- **Month**: Last 30 days of checks
- **Year**: Last 365 days of checks

## Pagination

The check table supports pagination to handle large amounts of data efficiently:

- Choose rows per page: 5, 10, 25, 50, or 100
- Navigate between pages using the arrow buttons
- The total check count is displayed above the table

## Sorting

Checks are sorted by date in descending order by default, showing the newest checks first. This helps you quickly see the most recent monitoring results.
